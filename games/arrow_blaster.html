Arrow Blaster
Arrow Blaster is an uncomplicated game. Aim, shoot and go... Your only goal will be to resist as long as you can and get the highest score possible.


MAIN FEATURES:
  • Action and fun in equal parts in the purest retro style.
  • Highly simple control: shoot and dash.
  • Incredible music by Alexandr Zhelanov.
