ABOUT US
bitmagine Studio is an indie studio focused on the making of videogames for Windows, Linux, Android and Browser, although we can also see more than one of their creations for MacOS.

Based in Zaragoza (Spain), the studio has several games published and several more in development, all of them developed by a single person who has been in the world of computers and games since the '80s, specifically from the Sinclair ZX Spectrum... later came Commodore Amiga and PC.

So far we have only published games on Itch.io and Google Play, but is still looking to find a publisher for PC/Steam and consoles, as well as the opportunity to enter the Asian market.

If you want us to publish our games with your company, please contact us.
